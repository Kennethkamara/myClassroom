# How to Enter Your Own Data - Quick Guide

## Step 1: Clear the Mock Data

1. Open this file in your browser: `reset-data.html` (in the cmt folder)
2. Click the red **"Clear All Mock Data"** button
3. Confirm when asked
4. You'll see a success message âœ…

**What this does:** Removes all the 30 sample students and their marks, so you start with an empty system.

---

## Step 2: Add Your Own Students

1. Open `index.html` (the main application)
2. Go to the **Students** tab (second tab with ğŸ‘¥ icon)
3. Click the green **"Add Student"** button
4. Fill in:
   - **Student Name:** e.g., "Ibrahim Koroma"
   - **Class:** Select JSS 1 (or JSS 2)
5. Click **"Save"**
6. **Repeat** for all your students

**Tip:** Add all students first before entering marks!

---

## Step 3: Configure Your Term Settings

1. Go to the **Configuration** tab (first tab with âš™ï¸ icon)
2. Select:
   - **Class:** e.g., JSS 1
   - **Subject:** e.g., Integrated Science
   - **Term:** e.g., 1st Term
3. Set:
   - **Test Marked Over:** e.g., 100 (or 60, 50, etc.)
   - **Maximum Added Marks:** e.g., 20 (how much bonus you can give)
4. Click **"Save Configuration"**

---

## Step 4: Enter Your Marks

1. Go to the **Mark Entry** tab (third tab with ğŸ“ icon)
2. Select the same:
   - **Class:** JSS 1
   - **Subject:** Integrated Science
   - **Term:** 1st Term
3. Click **"Load Marks"**
4. You'll see your students in a table!
5. Click on any cell and type the marks
6. The **Final Contribution** calculates automatically!
7. Click **"Save All Marks"** when done

---

## Step 5: Export or Print

1. Go to **Export & Print** tab
2. Select your class, subject, and term
3. Click:
   - **Export as Excel** - for your computer records
   - **Print Results** - for paper copy (Added Mark column hidden!)
